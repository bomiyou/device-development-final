Mate

Smart Motorcycle Helmet

What?

“Mate” is a smart helmet that can be your perfect riding mate. Mate is designed to do two simple things – keep you safe and be your friend during a ride. This helmet helps you navigate, helps you stay aware of your surroundings, helps you stay in touch with other people, entertain you and record your ride so that you can keep all those beautiful memories. The concept enables a safer and more fun experience for users who enjoy riding regularly.

Ideation +
Research

The idea of “mate” begins with the theme of a smart hat. This project was about designing a smart hat with proper branding and UX/UI. The first idea that came to my mind was a smart motorcycle helmet. Because my family loves to ride a motorcycle and I remember they always go ride on the weekend. I started this project thinking of my family and my goal was to create a helmet that they would need. To address and design for a smart helmet, I proceeded by generating all the ideas to explore various potential solutions.

To better understand and gain empathy for our target group, I proceeded in conducting a survey. I learned about users’ pain point from the survey, and I organized what features they needs to improve their experience.

Analyze

From all of this data, I created user personas to get better understanding about our potential users. After that, I put myself in Mark’s shoes, and create an user jouney map to see if this app meets user’s needs.
